A polyphonic synthesizer that runs entirely in your browser. Play with your computer keyboard or click the on-screen piano. Range expands from one octave to the full 88-key concert grand. Mappings are remappable and stick around between sessions.

Polyphonic keyboard

Every key is its own voice. Hold ten at once. Sine, square, sawtooth, triangle.

Full ADSR

Attack, decay, sustain, release. Sweep through pads, plucks, organs, leads.

Range expansion

1 octave through the full 88-key piano. Compact / fit / scrollable / full views.

Custom mapping

Click a piano key, press a computer key — it's bound. Saves to your browser.

Default keyboard layout

 C4 D4 E4 F4 G4 A4 B4 C5 D5 E5 F5
  A  S  D  F  G  H  J  K  L  ;  '   ← white keys
  W  E    T  Y  U     O  P          ← black keys

  Z = octave −     X = octave +
